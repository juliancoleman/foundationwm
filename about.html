about

the foundation wealth management, llc is a full service Financial Planning and Wealth Management firm. Founded in April of 2018 by Timothy F. Lourenco, the firm has one simple goal in mind, create value. Retirement planning, investments, and taxes are issues that are daunting to so many people. We ease that burden by providing simple to understand, unbiased, expert advice. Simplifying complicated issues, and providing valuable information is our priority with every client. We want to work with clients their way, in a manner that they feel comfortable with. We start every relationship by asking what we can do for you.

team

timothy f. lourenco, CFP* was born and raised in the Central Valley. Tim grew up in Coalinga, CA where he attended Coalinga High School and went on to attend West Hills College Coalinga on a full scholarship. While living in Coalinga, Tim worked for his family’s business from the age of 16 (officially). Being involved in a successful family business, provided key lessons and principles that he still carries with him today. After graduating from West Hills, he attended Fresno State where he studied and achieved a B.S. Business Administration – Finance degree. In 2012 Tim began his journey in financial services while interning at Waddell and Reed. He would go on to become fully licensed (Series 7, 66, California Life and Health License) and become employed by the firm. Tim later joined Principal Financial Group. After working in the financial services industry for over 5 years, it was evident that it was time to create something different. He left and founded The Foundation Wealth Management, LLC based on principles practiced and learned throughout his entire career. “Being in small town, with a lot of competition, it was crucial to put the customers first and always do what is right.”

Tim is a Certified Financial Planner™ (CFP*). The professional designation comes with extensive training in financial planning, estate planning, insurance, investments, taxes, employee benefits, and retirement planning, as well as in CFP Board's Standards of Professional Conduct, which are rigorously enforced.

Tim is an active member in the Saint Agnes Men’s Club, a Fresno area charitable organization benefiting Saint Agnes Hospital. He is also involved with the Alzheimer’s Association often fund raising, speaking at, or attending their events.

In his free time Tim enjoys spending time with his fiancée, Nabila. He enjoys traveling, snowboarding, golf, and time with family and friends.
